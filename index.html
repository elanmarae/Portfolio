The Bridges of Western Arizona

This project maps bridges and elevation data for Western Arizona using Mapbox.
Bridges extracted from the Domestic and Antarctic Names (State and Topical Gazetteer) published by the United States Board on Geographic Namkes. (geonames.usgs.gov) Data accessed March, 2019. Contours were extracted from a USGS NED 1/3 arc-second raster digital elevation model. This data can be accessed at the USGS 3D Elevation Program (usgs.gov)

See the full map here

South West Yuma and MCAS Yuma Parks

This map is intended to show parks in Southwestern Yuma that are close to the Marine Corps Air Station (MCAS Yuma). All parks are surrounded by a one mile radius to show proximity. This map also shows that all parks on base are within walking distance of base housing.
All data curated through OpenStreetMaps

A high-resolution version of this map can be accessed here.

About me

From The Shape of Design by Frank Chimero - "Marshall McLuhan said that, “we look at the present through a rear-view mirror,” and we “march backwards into the future.” Invention becomes our lens to imagine what is possible, and design is the road we follow to reach it. But, there is a snag in McLuhan’s view, because marching is no way to go into the future. It is too methodical and restricted. The world often subverts our best laid plans, so our road calls for a way to move that is messier, bolder, more responsive. The lightness and joy afforded by creating suggests that we instead dance."

Hi, I'm Elan. I'm a historian and librarian working with web development and cartography for digital access and digital humanities! I'm a bit of a romantic and a dreamer, and aesthetic is my greatest goal and my biggeset downfall. Ah the pursuit of perfection!

When I'm not banging my head against the computer I'm either reading, riding horses, learning to scuba dive, or hanging with my dog and husband USMC style.
