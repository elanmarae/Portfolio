A New Home for Scots

Following religious upheaval in Scotland in the 1600s, the Ward family made it's way to the New World, working their way through Virginia, and eventually setlling in the Ohio/Kentucky Region. Here many of the family lines would stay until present; through Independence, frontier living, and fighting on both sides in the American Civil War. Today, we branch out a little further and travel the world, but we always seem to come back to that good old home, Kentucky.
